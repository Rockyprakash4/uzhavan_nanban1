Pest Risk Alert — Action Required
High blast fungus risk detected in Thanjavur district. Current weather conditions (humidity 68%, temperature 28°C) are creating ideal conditions for Magnaporthe oryzae.

Recommended Action: Apply Tricyclazole 75% WP at 0.6 g/litre or Isoprothiolane 40% EC at 1.5 ml/litre. Spray in the evening between 5–7 PM for best results. Repeat after 10 days if conditions persist.
🌿
Brown Plant Hopper — Management Guide
BPH (Nilaparvata lugens) sucks plant sap and causes "hopperburn" — circular patches of dead plants. Avoid excess nitrogen. Use light traps to monitor populations.

Chemical Control: Buprofezin 25 SC at 1.0 ml/litre or Thiamethoxam 25 WG at 0.2 g/litre when population exceeds 10 per hill.
